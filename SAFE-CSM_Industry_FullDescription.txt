============================================================
1. Introduction
============================================================

The SAFE-CSM (Industry Edition) is a non-adaptive, external observation framework designed to evaluate contextual stability in industrial environments.
It does not access or analyze internal AI mechanisms and relies solely on observable external signals.

This edition supports analysis in industrial dashboards, scoring pipelines, and monitoring systems.


============================================================
2. Design Philosophy
============================================================

- External-only observation  
- Non-adaptive, fixed-parameter behavior  
- Simple Δφ index  
- Minimal, predefined correction  
- SAFE-layer alignment  


============================================================
3. Ten-Factor Model
============================================================

Ten external indicators:

  J1–J10

are used to form a multi-dimensional observable space.  
They are abstract, bounded in [-1, 1], and unrelated to internal AI computation.


============================================================
4. Observable Index Δφ
============================================================

Δφ is computed as:

    Δφ = J3 – J6

This index represents observable imbalance.  
“Affect” and “Reflex” are symbolic identifiers only.


============================================================
5. Reloop / Balance
============================================================

Δφ is considered stable within:

- LOWER = -0.3  
- UPPER = 0.3  

When Δφ exits this range, a small fixed correction is applied.  
The correction does not adapt, learn, or depend on history.


============================================================
6. Demonstration Flow
============================================================

1. Generate J1–J10  
2. Compute Δφ  
3. Compute J_total  
4. Apply SAFE-CSM correction  
5. Plot Δφ with/without correction  


============================================================
7. SAFE Compliance
============================================================

SAFE-CSM (Industry Edition):

- Does not access internal AI mechanisms  
- Does not infer or tune internal states  
- Does not learn or optimize  
- Operates only on fixed rules  
- Uses randomness exclusively for demonstration  
- Provides observation-only evaluation  

Implementation support for SAFE-CSM (Industry Edition) requires an NDA to prevent misconfiguration or unintended use.
